Why

We're inspired by the possibilities chatbots represent. We believe an early entrance in the chatbot era will open new customer segments and build closer relationships to customers. Research have shown that 30% of online time is spent on social media platforms. We believe the opportunity to reach users in this domain, without forcing them to exit the platform represents an important competitive edge.

What it does

The Chatbot responds to natural language to guide the potential customer in the process of buying clothes. In the end it will hopefully end in a confident and happy customer buying new clothes from Zalando.

+

How we built it

We used the We used the Microsoft Bot Builder framework with LUIS and combined it with the zalando API. We used cards to showcase the products and Cortana to guide the user on the journey.

For the future...

We believe the application of bots and personalization will be a huge competitive value in the future. By combining user research with machine learning on specific customer segments we can further improve convertion rates. The bot will need further work both in these segments.

Some toughts on design

The field of bots are still quite new, so the guidelines are still at an early stage. However some of them have already proven their point. The article Principles of bot design from the Intercom blog is a good introduction to some guidelines.
